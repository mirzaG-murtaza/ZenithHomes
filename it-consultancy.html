How often should I replace my water filter?

It is recommended that you replace your water filter every 6-12 months, depending on the type of filter and the amount of usage. Some filters may need to be changed more frequently if you have a large household or if your water supply is particularly contaminated.

Can a water filter remove all contaminants from my water?

While a high-quality water filter can remove many contaminants from your water, it may not be able to remove all of them. You can contact our customer service for more information.

What is the difference between a countertop and under-sink water filter?

A countertop water filter sits on top of your counter and connects directly to your faucet, while an under-sink water filter is installed under your sink and filters water before it reaches the faucet. Countertop filters are generally more portable and easier to install, while under-sink filters can be more effective and out of sight.

How do I know if my water filter is working properly?

You can test the effectiveness of your water filter by using a water testing kit. You can also monitor the taste and odor of your water to see if there are any noticeable changes. If you suspect that your filter is not working properly, it is best to contact our customer service so we can provide you with a solution.

Are water filters environmentally friendly?

Yes, water filters can be a more environmentally friendly option compared to bottled water. By using a water filter, you can reduce the amount of plastic waste generated from disposable water bottles. Additionally, some water filters can be recycled, further reducing their environmental impact.
