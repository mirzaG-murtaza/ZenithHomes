How long do solar panels last?

Solar panels have a long lifespan, typically lasting 25-30 years or more with proper maintenance. However, their efficiency may decrease slightly over time. Our team can provide ongoing maintenance and monitoring to ensure your solar system is operating at maximum efficiency.

Can solar panels work during a power outage?

It depends on the type of solar system you have. If you have a grid-tied system, it will not work during a power outage as it is designed to shut down for safety reasons. However, if you have a battery backup system or a hybrid solar system, it can continue to operate during a power outage, providing you with reliable backup power.

How much maintenance do solar panels require?

Solar panels are relatively low-maintenance, requiring only periodic cleaning and inspection to ensure they are operating at maximum efficiency. Our team can provide ongoing maintenance services to ensure your solar system is performing optimally.

Will solar panels increase the value of my property?

Yes, installing solar panels can increase the value of your property. Studies have shown that homes with solar panels can sell for more than those without, providing a strong return on investment.
